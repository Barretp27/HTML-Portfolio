My Top 3 Books of all-time.

Becoming Supernatural

Book by Joe Dispenza where he explains the how the mind and body work. How your personal reality is based on how you think, feel, and act. He connects neuro science and quantum physics to explain the unified field around the human body. He talks about heart and brain coherence and how it related to healing and manifestation.

The Untethered Soul

This book is written by Michael Singer and goes deep into the mind, body, and energy.

How to Win Friends and Influence People

This book is great. It gives you ways of talking to people and shows you how to make friends and inflence people to your side of things
