The Best Movies According to Barret

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Independence Day

This 1996 film is action packed with good dialogue and sci-fi elements. Will Smith and Jeff Goldblum star together in this one.

The Witcher (series)

Really cool sci-fi TV show with one of my favorite actors, Henry Cavil. This 3+ series contents awesome visuals, realistic fight choreography, and elements of magic!

Green Snake

Super beautifully animated film. Really artistic.
